About

Software Engineer

From an early age, my fascination with technology ignited when I delved into the realm of game servers and developed Java plugins as a freelancer. This experience sparked my interest in software engineering, driving me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. Throughout my academic journey, I have honed my skills in creating efficient code through courses like Data Structures and Design/Analysis of Algorithms.

As I progressed, I ventured into technology-focused roles, such as serving as a Technician for the IT department and the Makerspace at my school. These experiences provided hands-on opportunities to troubleshoot and resolve technical challenges, deepening my understanding of real-world applications.

Proficient in an array of programming languages including C++, C, C#, Java, Python, and web development technologies like HTML, CSS, PHP, and JavaScript, I possess a versatile skill set. Additionally, my expertise spans various operating systems such as Linux and Windows, as well as game engines like Unity and Unreal Engine. I have also worked with tools like Docker, ASP.NET, and AWS Server.

Approaching problem-solving with a meticulous mindset, I excel at dissecting high-level designs and delving into the finer details. This meticulous approach enables me to create effective and robust solutions.

What sets me apart extends beyond technical skills. My unique philosophical viewpoint, shaped by growing up with technology and embracing Object-Oriented knowledge, has instilled in me a simulation-like perspective of the world. This mindset fuels my creativity and drives me to think beyond conventions, seeking innovative ways to tackle challenges.

Outside of my professional pursuits, I also enjoy playing Valorant, a team-based competitive game. Through my experience in Valorant, I have learned the value of teamwork, communication, and strategic thinking. These skills have proven invaluable in collaborative projects and have enhanced my ability to work effectively in cross-functional teams.

Looking ahead, I aspire to work with cutting-edge technologies such as AI, VR, and the fascinating realm of Web3. Staying informed about the latest trends, I actively engage with social media platforms and conduct research to explore captivating technologies.
